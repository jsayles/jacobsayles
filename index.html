Jacob Sayles

Bio

Jacob Sayles started Office Nomads in Seattle in 2007 with business partner Susan Evans. Jacob loves nothing more than acting as a coworking ambassador, both internationally and locally in the Pacific Northwest. It allows him to combine his love of travel with his passion for coworking. He's visited spaces in New York, Philadelphia, Chicago, San Francisco, Las Angeles, Austin, Vancouver, London, Brussels, Amsterdam, Berlin, and Rio de Janeiro, forging connections and sharing experiences with each coworking community. In the Pacific Northwest he's encouraged the creation of several new coworking spaces and formed Coworking Seattle, which explores the wider coworking movement in a local setting. He is an active contributor on the international coworking group, a moderator for the coworking wiki, and is the manager of Nadine, an open source coworking software project. Beyond his work at Office Nomads, Jacob's current focus is to provide consultation for new projects and to facilitate greater collaboration between all spaces through resources like coworking.com and the wiki.
